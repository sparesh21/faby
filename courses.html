4 Weeks Professional Makeup

4 weeks professional make up training course is designed for those who are looking to start a career in make up or those who wish to further develop existing professional make up skills.
There is so much to discover in the world of make up and no training course can claim to teach you everything, be it 4 weeks, eight months or eight years! Building a successful make up career takes a lot of hard work; it’s not all glamorous people and locations! If however, like me, you have found a passion for make up, I can teach you the skills and knowledge that you will need to master, in order to embark on a career in this demanding industry.
The professional training course will provide a detailed introduction to key areas of skin care and make up. We provide a firm foundation in international techniques and etiquette.

WHAT WILL YOU LEARN IN 4 WEEKS?M

  1. 1. Brush types and uses
  2. 2. Product knowledge and setting up
  3. 3. Prepping your artist / client / model
  4. 4. Colour theory
  5. 5. Working with different skin tones and textures
  6. 6. Foundation applications / concealing techniques
  7. 7. Contouring techniques - working with highlight and contour
  8. 8. False lashes application
  9. 9. Natural / Neutral Make up
  10. 10. Glamour Make up
  11. 11. Catwalk / Theatre Make up
  12. 12. Fashion / Editorial Make-up
  13. 13. Bridal Makeup
